Project Title

An Unreal Engine 5 material inspired by the cosmetic shader system from 'Destiny 2'. In 'Destiny 2' the player can collect shaders to apply to the cosmetics worn and used by their player character. These shaders give the appearance of different metals and fabrics on the players equipment. Each metal and fabric type is assigned it's material parameters seperately within the parameters screen of the material instance. I also used C++ to create a component for cosmetics, and added functionality that would allow the changing of these materials at runtime.

My implementation switches between 4 sets of material properties based on a colour provided; red and green to metals and blue and yellow to fabrics. This could be achieved using vertex colours, though is currently applied via a texture sample.
