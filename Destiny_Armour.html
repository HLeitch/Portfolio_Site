Project Title

An Unreal Engine 5 material inspired by the cosmetic shader system from 'Destiny 2'. In 'Destiny 2' the player can collect shaders to apply to the cosmetics worn and used by their player character. These shaders give the appearance of different metals and fabrics on the players equipment. At runtime, this is a quick and effective way to grant the player customisablity as a reward for progressing, though it would require a great deal of communication to execute in a large development team.

My implementation switches between 4 sets of material properties based on a colour provided; red and green to metals and blue and yellow to fabrics. This could be achieved using vertex colours, though is currently applied via a texture sample.
